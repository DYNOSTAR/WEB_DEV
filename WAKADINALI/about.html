ABOUT

Wakadinali aka RONG RENDE is a Kenyan HipHop group consisting of SCAR, DOMANI and SEWER SYDAA hailing from Eastlands. With their hard-core style, reality-based rap and immaculate flows, Wakadinali are easy contenders for anchoring the hip-hop scene in Kenya for a new era.
